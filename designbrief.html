BIOGRAPHY

US Pop Rock band U Might Be Mine has received critical acclaim from across the world for their debut album "Not So Rational." Set out to tour throughout North America and Europe, with performances at the Staples Center in Los Angeles and Madison Square Garden in New York City.

Growing up in the middle of nowhere New Jersey, members Dan Abbaticchio (Guitar), Jack Powers (Drums), Gabby Guida (Lead Singer), and Juliette O'Hara (on bass) all from a young age knew that their calling in life was their predominant love, music. Being involved in music ever since they were kids, actually being able to perform (especially in a full blown band) was a hopeful but distant dream at the same time. Some might say, meeting eachother in 2017 was fate coming together to make these kids' dreams come true.

With a full set album with songs like "Honey!," "Let Me Know When I'm Good Enough," and "Untitled," U Might Be Mine is ready to take the world by storm (in music of course!).
